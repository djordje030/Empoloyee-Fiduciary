The 401(k) Pitfalls That Delay Retirement

To retire as soon as possible, you must avoid three 401(k) pitfalls throughout your working years:

High fees

By the time you retire, high fees can cost you hundreds of thousands of dollars in lost principal and compound interest.

Underperforming investments

Studies show index funds tend to outperform comparable active funds over time, net-of-fees. You should earn no less.

Inappropriate asset allocation

Investing too conservatively when young can cost you gains, while investing too aggressively when older can cost you unrecoverable losses.
